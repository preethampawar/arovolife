This Direct Seller Agreement and Terms of Service (the "Agreement") is the master contract between **Arovolife Private Limited**, a company incorporated under the Companies Act 2013 (CIN U46909TS2026PTC210896), having its registered office in Hyderabad, Telangana, India ("arovolife", "Company", "we", "us") and the individual whose registration is completed under it ("Direct Seller", "Distributor", "you"). The Agreement becomes binding when an arovolife Distributor Number ("ADN") is issued at the end of registration, and remains binding until terminated under §11.

Acceptance is recorded electronically. The acceptance log captures the version number of this Agreement (as displayed at the top of the registration screen), the timestamp, the IP address, the device fingerprint, the OTP-verified mobile number, the typed full name and the PAN of the Direct Seller. This electronic record is a valid contract under §10A of the Information Technology Act 2000 and §65B of the Indian Evidence Act 1872.

## 1. Definitions

In this Agreement, unless context otherwise requires:

- **"ADN"** means the arovolife Distributor Number — the permanent unique identifier issued at the end of registration.
- **"Agreement"** means this Direct Seller Agreement & Terms of Service as published at `/p/terms`, together with the Code of Ethics (`/p/ethics`), the Privacy Policy (`/p/privacy`), the Grievance Redressal Policy (`/p/grievance`), and the published Compensation Plan disclosure.
- **"BV"** (Business Volume) means the volume points attached to each SKU and used by the compensation engine to compute downline overrides.
- **"Cooling-Off Period"** means the 30 calendar days commencing on the Effective Date, during which the Direct Seller may cancel without cause.
- **"Couple Distributorship"** means a single ADN jointly held by two consenting spouses (a "primary" and a "secondary" holder).
- **"DSR 2021"** means the Consumer Protection (Direct Selling) Rules 2021 issued under the Consumer Protection Act 2019.
- **"DPDP Act"** means the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.
- **"Effective Date"** means the date on which the ADN is issued.
- **"End-Consumer"** means a person who buys an arovolife product for personal use and not for resale.
- **"KYC"** means Know-Your-Customer — the document verification process described in §8.
- **"Line Change Window"** has the meaning given in §3.5.
- **"PAN"** means the Permanent Account Number issued by the Income Tax Department of India.
- **"Placement"** means the position of a Distributor in the binary tree.
- **"Placement Strategy"** means the Company-wide admin setting (`default_left`, `default_right`, or `custom`) that decides the starting leg when a placement is chosen. See ADR-0002.
- **"PV"** (Personal Volume) means the volume points generated by the Distributor's own retail purchases of arovolife product.
- **"Repurchase Logic"** means the requirement that ranks, payouts and pool participation are conditional on a minimum recurring PV in qualifying months.
- **"Sponsor"** means the Distributor through whom you joined, as recorded in the horizontal sponsorship tree.

## 2. Eligibility

You may register as a Direct Seller only if:

1. **You are a natural person** at least eighteen (18) years of age. In the State of Maharashtra the minimum age is twenty-one (21) years. State-wise overrides are configured in arovolife's admin settings and may change without notice to reflect statutory amendments; the value applied to your registration is the one in force at the time you submit the eligibility step.
2. **You are an Indian citizen or a person legally resident in India** with a valid PAN and a valid Aadhaar (or, where applicable, an equivalent identity document approved by the Company).
3. **You have not been declared insolvent**, have not been convicted of an offence involving moral turpitude in the preceding five (5) years, and are not currently barred from doing direct-selling business by any regulator.
4. **You have not previously held an ADN** which was terminated for cause and remains in a cooling period.
5. **You have a working mobile number** and a working email address that you control.
6. **You have a bank account in your own name** (or jointly with your spouse, for Couple Distributorships) into which payouts can be credited.
7. **You are not an employee or admin of arovolife**, or a relative as defined in §2(77) of the Companies Act 2013 of such an employee, unless arovolife's HR policy expressly permits the arrangement and the corresponding declaration is recorded in your KYC.
8. **You are not registered in another active arovolife ADN** (one PAN = one ADN; see §3.4).

Failure to meet any eligibility criterion, whether discovered at registration or later, voids the Agreement ab initio and authorises arovolife to terminate the ADN without further notice.

## 3. Registration

### 3.1 Free of Cost

Registration is **free of cost**. arovolife does not charge any joining fee, registration fee, kit fee, training fee, examination fee, deposit, "starter pack" or similar amount as a condition of issuing an ADN. Any person demanding such a payment in arovolife's name is acting without authority; report them to `/contact-us` immediately. *(Hard rule #1; DSR 2021 Rule 5(1)(a).)*

### 3.2 Orientation Requirement

Before an ADN is issued, you must complete a mandatory orientation comprising:

1. A video module covering arovolife's business model, the prohibition on income projections, the cooling-off period, the Code of Ethics, and the grievance process. You must view at least **95%** of the module duration; rapid scrubbing is detected and disqualifies the session.
2. A micro-quiz of multiple-choice questions drawn from the orientation content. You must score at least the configured pass mark (currently 70%).

The orientation result, including the video watch percentage, the quiz answers and the device on which it was completed, is recorded against your registration.

### 3.3 KYC Submission

You must submit the documents listed in §8 (KYC). Verification is performed by arovolife and by a UIDAI-approved AUA/KUA partner for Aadhaar. The ADN is issued only after KYC reaches a verified state.

### 3.4 One PAN = One ADN

Each PAN may hold exactly one active ADN. A Couple Distributorship is a single ADN with two consenting holders; each spouse's PAN is recorded against the same ADN with a primary/secondary flag and may not be used for any other ADN. Attempted registration of a second ADN against an existing PAN is automatically rejected. *(Hard rule #6.)*

### 3.5 Line Change Window

Within five (5) working days of the Effective Date, and **only if** you have neither sponsored another Distributor nor placed any qualifying product order, you may request a one-time change of your sponsor or your placement. The request is logged, reviewed and (if approved) executed by arovolife within ten working days. After the Line Change Window closes, your sponsor and placement are final, subject only to rare administrative corrections logged as audit entries.

### 3.6 Electronic Record

You agree that the registration session, including every acceptance click, OTP verification, KYC upload, orientation watch event and quiz answer, constitutes an electronic record within the meaning of the Information Technology Act 2000, that this electronic record is admissible evidence of your acceptance, and that you waive any objection to its admissibility on the ground that it is in electronic form.

## 4. Cooling-Off Period

### 4.1 Right to Cancel

You have an unconditional right to cancel this Agreement at any time during the Cooling-Off Period, with or without cause, by clicking "Cancel my registration" inside your dashboard, or by writing to `cooling-off@arovolife.in` (TBD — replace with real address before launch) from your registered email. No reason need be stated.

### 4.2 Effect of Cancellation

Upon cancellation during the Cooling-Off Period:

1. Your ADN is immediately deactivated.
2. Any product purchased by you in your ADN's name is eligible for full refund. You must return the product in re-saleable condition to the address provided by arovolife within fourteen (14) days of cancellation; the cost of return shipping is borne by arovolife.
3. The refund (less any non-refundable government taxes that arovolife cannot reclaim) is credited to your registered bank account within seven (7) business days of arovolife receiving the returned product (or, for digital-only orders, within seven business days of cancellation).
4. Any BV/PV generated by you, and any commission credited to your upline as a consequence of your purchase, is reversed.
5. Your downline (if any) is preserved and reassigned to your sponsor or to a compliant alternative position determined by the placement engine.

### 4.3 Reminders

arovolife sends SMS and email reminders of the Cooling-Off Period at **D-20**, **D-7** and **D-1** before expiry. The reminders include a one-tap cancellation link. *(Hard rule #5; T&C §4.)*

### 4.4 No Waiver

You cannot waive the Cooling-Off Period in advance. Any clause, oral or written, in any other document purporting to do so is void.

## 5. Direct Selling Model

### 5.1 Direct-to-Consumer Only

arovolife operates exclusively as a direct-selling entity. Products are sold to End-Consumers through Distributors, attributed via the ADN, with full traceability. *(Hard rule #7; DSR 2021 Rule 4.)*

### 5.2 Prohibited Sales Channels

You may not:

1. List or cause to be listed arovolife products on any third-party e-commerce marketplace, including but not limited to Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra, JioMart, Snapdeal, Tata Cliq, Nykaa, Ajio, FirstCry, BigBasket, Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, Instagram Shop, Facebook Marketplace, OLX, Quikr, or any platform that accepts orders without the customer being attributed to a specific ADN at the moment of purchase.
2. Stock arovolife product in a physical retail store, kirana, pharmacy, salon, gym, clinic, school or college canteen for over-the-counter resale.
3. Wholesale arovolife product to any reseller, distributor, broker, or aggregator who is not themselves an active arovolife Distributor.
4. Cross-border export or import without the Company's prior written authorisation.

Violation is a major breach under the Code of Ethics §6 and may lead to immediate suspension or termination, irrespective of the volume or revenue involved.

### 5.3 Daily Order Caps & Attribution

To prevent inventory loading and surrogate selling, arovolife enforces:

- a **daily per-Distributor purchase cap** (configurable; current value visible in your dashboard);
- a **per-ADN customer attribution rule** — every customer order must carry exactly one Distributor's ADN, captured via the Distributor's referral link, dashboard or in-person order entry;
- a **15-day attribution window** for guest-checkout customers — a guest who buys via a referral link is attributed to that Distributor for any repeat purchases within the next 15 days, after which the attribution lapses unless the customer logs in and explicitly retains it.

### 5.4 Returns & Refunds

The full Returns & Refunds Policy is published at `/p/returns` (to be created in Phase 2). Until that page is live, the following minimum guarantees apply, in addition to the cooling-off rights in §4:

- **Customer return window:** 15 calendar days from delivery, for unopened products in resaleable condition.
- **Defective / damaged-on-arrival:** full refund or replacement at customer's choice within 7 days of reported delivery; return shipping borne by arovolife.
- **Buyback for Distributors:** on termination for any reason, arovolife will repurchase resaleable, unexpired stock at not less than ninety per cent (90%) of the price paid by the Distributor, less reasonable handling, in accordance with DSR 2021 Rule 7. Stock returned must be in saleable condition and within 90 days of termination.

## 6. Compensation Framework

### 6.1 No Commission Without a Product Sale

You earn commissions, bonuses, pool credits, ranks, or any other monetary or in-kind reward **only as a function of qualifying retail product sales** to End-Consumers, captured in arovolife's commerce system with a verifiable `product_sale_id`. You earn **nothing** for the act of recruiting another Distributor. *(Hard rule #2; DSR 2021 Rule 5(1)(c).)*

### 6.2 Plan Disclosure

The current compensation plan — including BV per SKU, rank thresholds, payout slabs, caps and the formula used to compute pool shares — is published at `/p/compensation` (to be created in Phase 4) and is updated only with 30 days' written notice to all active Distributors. The Company-wide settings that drive the engine (e.g. Placement Strategy, daily order cap, payout cadence) are visible in your dashboard's "Compliance & Plan" section.

### 6.3 No Income Projections

The Company does not, and you may not, publish, project, suggest, average, illustrate or imply any specific income that a Distributor may earn from arovolife. Only the speaker's own historical earnings, with full context of effort and capital, may be discussed. *(Hard rule #3.)*

### 6.4 Rank Maintenance ("PYP")

Ranks are awarded based on qualifying BV and PV in a defined accounting period. To retain a rank in subsequent periods, the Distributor must continue to meet a minimum repurchase requirement ("PYP" — Prove Your Position) configured in the published plan. Failure to maintain results in automatic rank step-down at the next period close, with written notice and a 30-day grace period to recover.

### 6.5 Commission Caps

To prevent unsustainable payouts, arovolife enforces hard caps on:

- the maximum daily payout per ADN;
- the percentage of the Company's monthly product revenue that may be distributed as commissions;
- the maximum override depth in the binary leg (currently capped per the published plan).

Capped amounts are not "owed" or "carried forward"; they are absorbed by the customer-protection and infrastructure reserves of the Company.

## 7. Tax & TDS

### 7.1 GST

Product prices include or exclude GST as displayed on each SKU page; the GST amount is itemised on every invoice. arovolife collects and deposits GST in accordance with the Central Goods and Services Tax Act 2017 and corresponding State Acts.

### 7.2 TDS on Commissions

arovolife deducts tax at source on commission payouts in accordance with the Income Tax Act 1961, including without limitation:

- **§194-H** (commission and brokerage), where the Distributor's annual commission crosses the prescribed threshold;
- **§194-O** (TDS on e-commerce participants), where applicable to a particular transaction structure;
- **§206AA** (higher rate where PAN is not furnished) and **§206AB** (higher rate for non-filers), where applicable.

Deducted tax is remitted to the Income Tax Department and reflected in your Form 26AS. A TDS certificate (Form 16A) is issued quarterly within the statutory timeline.

### 7.3 Distributor's Own Tax Obligations

You are responsible for your own income-tax filings, including reporting commission income, claiming credit for TDS, and (where applicable) obtaining and maintaining your own GST registration if your turnover crosses the statutory threshold. arovolife does not provide tax advice; consult a qualified Chartered Accountant.

## 8. KYC Obligations

### 8.1 Documents Collected

At registration and on each subsequent material change, arovolife collects:

| Category | Field | Verification |
|---|---|---|
| Identity | PAN (full 10-character number captured at submission) | Validated against Income Tax NSDL/UTI database (Phase 1: format validation only; Phase 2+: NSDL API). |
| Identity | Aadhaar (full 12-digit number captured at submission, in-memory only) | Verified through a UIDAI-approved AUA/KUA partner. After verification the full Aadhaar is **purged**; only a reference token and the last 4 digits are retained. |
| Identity | Full legal name | Cross-checked against PAN/Aadhaar; mismatches block KYC. |
| Identity | Date of birth | Cross-checked against PAN/Aadhaar; used for age-gate enforcement. |
| Contact | Mobile number (E.164) | OTP-verified at registration; one mobile may anchor only one ADN. |
| Contact | Email | OTP-verified at registration. |
| Address | Permanent address, state, pincode | Captured for jurisdictional rules; document proof required (Aadhaar address, voter ID, utility bill). |
| Bank | Account number, IFSC, account holder name | Penny-drop verified before any payout; name must match PAN holder. |

### 8.2 Storage of Sensitive Data

In accordance with the Privacy Policy and the DPDP Act:

- **PAN** is stored as a SHA-256 hash plus the last four characters in plaintext for display. Uniqueness checks operate on the hash. *(Hard rule #8.)*
- **Aadhaar full number** is held in memory only for the AUA/KUA verification call. Once the partner returns a verification token, the full number is destroyed. The system retains the token and the last four digits only.
- **Bank account number** is encrypted at rest using the platform's KMS-managed key; only the last four digits are displayed in the UI.
- **Uploaded KYC documents** (PAN PDF/JPEG, Aadhaar masked image, address proof, bank proof) are stored on encrypted object storage in the India region with private ACLs; access is time-limited and audit-logged.

### 8.3 Retention Period

| Data class | Retention from termination | Statutory basis |
|---|---|---|
| KYC documents | 8 years | Income Tax Act §44AA + DSR 2021 record-keeping. |
| Transactional records (orders, invoices, payouts) | 8 years | Income Tax Act §44AA + CGST Act §35–36. |
| Audit log of admin actions | 8 years | Internal compliance; DPDP Act §8. |
| Grievance tickets | 7 years | DSR 2021 Rule 12. |
| Marketing-consent contact data | 12 months from last interaction | DPDP Act minimisation principle. |
| Behavioural / session analytics | 24 months | DPDP Act minimisation principle. |

### 8.4 Right to Erasure

You may request erasure of any personal data held beyond the retention periods above by writing to the Data Protection Officer at `dpo@arovolife.in` (TBD — replace before launch). arovolife will respond within 30 days. Data retained under a legal obligation (e.g. tax records within the 8-year window) cannot be erased on request.

## 11. Term & Termination

### 11.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues in force unless terminated under this §11. Annual renewal of the ADN is a procedural step (re-acceptance of this Agreement, ethics affirmation, refreshed contact details); it does not start a new term.

### 11.2 Termination by You

You may terminate this Agreement:

1. During the Cooling-Off Period — see §4.
2. After the Cooling-Off Period — by giving 30 days' written notice to arovolife. Any unpaid earnings accrued up to the termination date are paid in the next regular payout cycle, subject to TDS and to the satisfaction of any outstanding obligations.

### 11.3 Termination by arovolife — for Cause

arovolife may terminate this Agreement immediately, with written notice, for any of the following:

- Breach of any of the hard rules listed in this Agreement or in the Code of Ethics;
- Material misrepresentation in your registration (false PAN, false age, false address, false bank details);
- Fraud, deception, or any conduct that exposes arovolife or its customers to financial, reputational or legal harm;
- Conviction of an offence involving moral turpitude;
- Insolvency, bankruptcy or any analogous proceeding against you;
- 12 consecutive months of zero PV activity, after a written notice and a 30-day cure period.

### 11.4 Termination by arovolife — Without Cause

arovolife may terminate this Agreement without cause on 90 days' written notice. In such case, your unpaid earnings are paid out in full at the next regular cycle and your unsold resaleable stock is bought back under §5.4.

### 11.5 Consequences of Termination

On termination, for any reason:

1. Your ADN is deactivated; you may no longer use it to place or attribute orders.
2. You must immediately cease using arovolife's trademarks, training material, customer lists and digital tools.
3. Confidentiality and post-termination obligations in §13 survive.
4. KYC and transactional data is retained under the schedule in §8.3.
5. Your downline is preserved and re-parented under arovolife's normal placement rules; you have no claim against arovolife or against the new parent on account of the re-parenting.

## 12. Suspension

arovolife may suspend your ADN, with written reasons, where:

- a grievance against you is under investigation;
- a regulator, law-enforcement agency or court has opened an inquiry that involves you;
- a security incident affecting your account is being investigated;
- a KYC re-verification is pending and the deadline has passed;
- unusual activity (anomalous BV spikes, repeated failed payouts, multiple device fingerprints, suspected account sharing) requires verification.

Suspension is reversible. Commissions earned during a suspension that is later lifted are credited at the next payout cycle. Suspension that is later converted to termination forfeits commissions attributable to the breach.
