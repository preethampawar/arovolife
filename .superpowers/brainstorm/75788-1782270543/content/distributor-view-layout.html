What does the distributor's earnings portal look like?

The distributor sees only their own Genos data. Where it lives and how it's structured:

A

Single "My Earnings" page with sections

One page under the main nav with all earnings sections stacked vertically: Personal BV & Title → Group BV (Left/Right today + carry-forward) → GSB History (table, paginated) → Mentorship Bonus → Wallet Balance → Payout History. Date filters apply globally across all sections.
