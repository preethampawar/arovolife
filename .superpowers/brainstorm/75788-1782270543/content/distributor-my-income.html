About this page
This dashboard shows a live snapshot of your Genos Income. Group BV updates as your Genos members make purchases throughout the day. The 23:59 daily cut-off locks the BV for that day and calculates your Genos Sales Bonus. Your wallet is credited after the cut-off and your earnings are transferred to your bank account every Tuesday. Deductions (3% admin charge, 5% TDS, and any repurchase wallet balance) are applied before transfer.

Tab 2 — Genos BV (daily group BV history)

Answers: "What BV did my left and right groups generate each day, and what carried forward?"
How this works: Every day at 23:59 the platform locks your Left and Right Genos BV. The weaker side (lower of the two) is matched against the 7 slabs and your personal title to determine your Genos Sales Bonus. After the match, the weaker side resets to zero. The stronger (power) side is carried forward, capped at 4,50,000 BV — any excess is flushed. For Slab 1 (15,000 BV match) only, your weaker side also carries forward indefinitely — there is no time limit to hit your first ₹1,000 Genos Sales Bonus.

About deductions
Your wallet receives GSB and Mentorship Bonus credits after each daily 23:59 cut-off. Every Tuesday, the wallet balance (minus deductions) is transferred to your registered bank account — provided the balance is at least ₹500. Repurchase deduction: 10% of your previous month's GSB + Mentorship Bonus (capped at ₹10,000) is held back to fund your mandatory monthly repurchase. Admin charge: 3% (or ₹30,000 — whichever is lower) is deducted before the credit reaches your wallet. TDS: 5% is deducted at source per Income Tax rules.
